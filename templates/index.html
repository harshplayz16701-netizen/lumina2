Frequently Asked Questions

Curious? Here are answers.

How accurate is the computer vision skin analysis? +

Our algorithms use specific color, pixel clusters, and variance filters to detect surface levels of sebum oil, red regions, and under-eye values. While it offers excellent clinical screening of basic skin conditions, it does not replace a professional medical diagnosis by a dermatologist.

Is my photo stored in the system? +

Yes. To allow you to access and review your premium digital skin score over time or share the link, a stylized copy of your photo with the scientific wireframe overlay is saved locally on our secure, encrypted server. You can request deletion at any time.

Why choose Lumina over standard AI recommendations? +

Generative AIs can hallucinate skincare ingredient cocktails that counteract or irritate your skin. Lumina Skin is fully database-driven, meaning every single routine and product recommendation has been safely hand-configured according to dermatological standards.
